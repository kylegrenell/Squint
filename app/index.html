SQUINT

Squint is a Scottish ready-to-wear clothing brand designed and manufactured in house in Edinburgh by Ellie Vallely.

Founded by Ellie in 2016 after graduating from Brighton University, Squint strives to create concept based functional clothing. The brand focuses on play, naivety of shape, colour and textile manipulation.

Questions, collaborations, commissions? Please get in touch on the contact page.

At Squint we strive to be an inclusive brand for all shapes and sizes! However at the early stage the brand is at, I am only able to supply S,M,L and One Size. I hope to expand my sizing as soon as I can and apologise to anyone who feels they are not being represented <3
